The transition property is a shorthand property for:
transition-property
transition-duration
transition-timing-function
transition-delay

eg.

     transition:  border-radius 2s ease 1s , background-color 2s ease-out 1s ;
    
                                ^^^^^^^^       ^^^^^  ^^^^^  ^^^^
                                property  -  duration- delay - timingFunction

transition-property
 the border-radius property   +   the background-color property.

transition-duration
 2s for border-radius  & 2s for background-color.

transition-delay
 1s for  border-radius & 2s for background-color.

transition-timing-function 
 ease for border-radius & ease-out for background-color.
